Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Trustnet ("we", "us") is a personal recommendation network: it lets you ask people you trust for recommendations and keep what they answer. This page explains what we store and how we use it. The short version: your data is yours, we don't sell it, and we don't show ads.

What we collect

  • Account details — your email address, name, and general location, provided by you at sign-up.
  • Your network — the circles you create and the contact details (name, phone number or email) of members you add. You are responsible for having their permission to add them.
  • Content — questions you ask, answers you and others give, items you save to your library, notes, ratings, and collections you create.
  • Messages you send to our WhatsApp number — used solely to save the content into your library and reply to you.
  • Technical basics — standard logs needed to run and secure the service.

How we use it

  • To run Trustnet: deliver your questions, collect answers, organize your library, and show your network's shared recommendations.
  • AI processing (e.g., OpenAI) is used to classify and organize the content you save. Content is processed for that purpose only.
  • Messages are delivered through WhatsApp (Meta), email (Resend), and in-app notifications. Delivery providers process the minimum needed to deliver.

What we never do

  • We never sell your data.
  • We never show ads or sponsored recommendations.
  • We never share your library or circles with anyone you didn't share them with yourself.

Sharing controls

Recommendations you save can be shared to your network feed — this is controlled per item and in your profile settings. Collections you create are visible to anyone who has their link. Everything else is private to you.

Storage & security

Data is stored with Supabase (managed Postgres) using industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest, with row-level access controls.

Your rights

You can ask us to export or permanently delete your account and data at any time — contact us at the address below and we'll do it promptly.
